FAQs

What is the best car for beginners?

For beginners, consider models like Honda Civic, Toyota Corolla, or Mazda3 due to their reliability and ease of handling.

How often should I service my car?

Service intervals depend on the manufacturer, but a general rule is every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first.

Which engine oil is best for high-performance cars?

Synthetic oils like Mobil 1 or Castrol EDGE are often recommended for high-performance cars.
